Meet Jerry

erry is a handsome male Beagle. He is very healthy, active, friendly, cheeky, and intelligent. He is a typical Beagle in some of his behaviours but completely different in others. Jerry has pet modelling experience & loves posing in front of the camera and is an extremely photogenic boy. He is a very lively fun-loving boy who is an ideal pet talent for a variety of roles. This handsome pet knows all agility training including going through tunnels and weaving through poles and taking direction well and from a distance. He also has the ability to learn a sequence of commands, enjoys training, and picks things up easily. New tricks aren’t hard for him to learn and he’s always up for learning something new. This clever little dog can respond to commands via voice and hand signals and also work with props with no issues.

Meet Tom

Tom is a bluish-grey and white anthropomorphic domestic short haired tuxedo British cat who first appeared in the 1940 MGM animated short Puss Gets the Boot. The cat was known as Jasper during his debut in the short; however, beginning with his next appearance in The Midnight Snack he was known as Tom or Thomas.
